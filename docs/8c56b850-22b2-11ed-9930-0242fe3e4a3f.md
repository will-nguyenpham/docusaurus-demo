# <a id="id" class="anchor_top_offset"/><a id="ariaid-title1" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Administrative Roles and Permissions

<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">​<span className="ph">Katalon Platform</span>​ organizes users into four management levels:</p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"><ul className="ul"><li className="li">Account</li><li className="li"><p className="p">Organization</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">Team</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">Project</p></li></ul></div>
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"> The account level is the highest, followed by the organization, the team, then the project level. </p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">Overall, the account level is in charge of subscription and billing management functions, while the organization level collates teams, projects, and users for collaboration.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"> Members can be assigned different roles with varying permissions and privileges. While they can be granted administrator rights, advanced administrative functions are inaccessible to them by default. </p>
